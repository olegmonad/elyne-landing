Универсальная стилистика (если не указано иное в слайде)

Эти 7 anchors должны быть в каждом visual-промте. Можно скопировать как «прелюдию» к каждому промту.

Square 1:1 aspect ratio, 1080x1080. Cinematic editorial photography, A24 film aesthetic, Apple product page mood. Dark warm charcoal background #0E0D0B, deep shadow detail. Single warm practical light source, 3000K, side or top-right. Subtle film grain, Kodak 500T feel, no digital sharpness. Warm shadows, slightly cool highlights, low contrast curve. Tactile real materials — aged wood, matte cast iron, linen, ceramic. Bradford Young cinematography reference, slow-cinema atmosphere. NO glossy, NO luxury, NO gold glitter, NO instagram aesthetic, NO vibrant saturation, NO clean white background, NO AI futurism, NO neon, NO gradient blobs, NO product packshot, NO motivational poster vibe.
